Dios is a module created by Magaman for Dante bots. It is also ported to other bots by members on dAmn.

Installation
  First Time Installation.
  Unzip the folder and place it into your "modules" folder inside your Dante bot.
 
  Updating.
  Delete the old Dios folder in your "modules" folder and replace it with the new version.
